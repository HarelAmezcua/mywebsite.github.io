about me

I'm a Robotics Engineer with a deep foundation in machine learning, computer vision, and advanced robotic systems.

Early in my career, I focused on building intelligent perception and control systems for autonomous platforms, applying ML to real-time decision-making in dynamic environments. This led me to pivot into data science, where I could apply my analytical skills more broadly—developing predictive models, performing complex analyses, and driving insight across domains. Over time, I recognized that deploying these intelligent systems effectively required more than just models—it demanded robust, scalable data infrastructure. That insight drove me to specialize in data engineering. I’ve since built end-to-end data pipelines that power machine learning workflows and analytics at scale, working with tools like Apache Airflow, Python, PostgreSQL, and cloud platforms.
